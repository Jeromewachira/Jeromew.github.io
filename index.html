About Me

My Journey from Electronics to Cybersecurity

My journey into cybersecurity began with a solid foundation in electronics engineering, where I developed a deep understanding of hardware systems and digital technologies.

After completing my BSc in Electronics, I discovered my passion for cybersecurity while working with embedded systems and IoT devices. The intersection of hardware and software security fascinated me, leading to a natural transition into the cybersecurity field.

Today, I'm actively expanding my expertise through various certifications and hands-on projects. My unique background in electronics gives me a comprehensive perspective on security - from hardware vulnerabilities to network protocols and digital forensics.

I believe in continuous learning and staying ahead of emerging threats. Whether it's setting up SIEM solutions, conducting digital forensics analysis, or exploring cloud security on AWS, I approach each challenge with curiosity and determination.
